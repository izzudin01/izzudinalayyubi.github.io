Customer Shopping Analysis

Analytics project to understand customer behavior using transaction data: segments, purchasing patterns, and practical recommendations.

Objective

  • Identify customer segments based on behavior (frequency/spend/basket).
  • Find high-contribution products and shopping patterns.
  • Recommend actions for retention and sales improvement.

Key Outputs

  • Customer segmentation (example: loyal vs occasional).
  • Basket / category contribution view.
  • Simple KPI summary for management review.

Approach

  • Data preparation: clean transactions, create customer-level features.
  • Exploration: distribution of spend, frequency, and category contribution.
  • Segmentation: rule-based or clustering (depending on dataset quality).
  • Translate findings into recommendations (pricing, bundling, retention).

Tools & Tech

Python Pandas EDA Segmentation Visualization
