Steam → Electric Cartridge Heater Retrofit

Industrial heating system retrofit feasibility study.

Objective

Evaluate technical and economic feasibility of replacing centralized steam heating with localized electric cartridge heaters to improve temperature stability and reduce operational cost.

My Contribution

  • Developed electrical integration concept and protection philosophy
  • Designed temperature control strategy (on-off → PI/PID time-proportional)
  • Built ROI / NPV / IRR financial model
  • Performed technical feasibility assessment

Impact

  • Improved mold temperature stability target: 164 ± 2°C
  • Provided structured investment justification for management decision
