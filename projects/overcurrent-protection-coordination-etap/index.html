Overcurrent Protection Coordination (ETAP)

Power system protection study focused on coordination logic (selectivity) to improve safety and reduce unnecessary trips.

GitHub Repo

Objective

  • Build a coordination approach so upstream/downstream protection behaves predictably.
  • Reduce outage impact by improving selectivity (trip only the faulted section when possible).

Why It Matters (Operations)

  • Better coordination reduces downtime from nuisance trips.
  • Clear protection philosophy improves maintainability and troubleshooting speed.
  • Supports safer operation and equipment protection.
