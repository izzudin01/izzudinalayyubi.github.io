Coffee Sales Trends Analysis

Data analysis project to identify sales patterns, top products, and time-based trends for business decision support.

Objective

  • Understand sales trend over time (daily/weekly/monthly).
  • Identify best-selling items and contribution to revenue.
  • Generate insights to support inventory and promotion planning.

Deliverables

  • Clean dataset + consistent features (time, product, quantity, sales).
  • Charts: trend, seasonality, top items, contribution.
  • Short recommendation summary (actionable points).

Approach

  • Data cleaning: handle missing values, date parsing, standardize categories.
  • Exploratory analysis: time-series trend, peak hours/days, product ranking.
  • Business framing: translate charts into operational actions (stock, staffing, promo timing).

Tools & Tech

Python Pandas Matplotlib EDA Reporting
